History of Medical stores:


Medical Stores Department (MSD) was established by the Act of Parliament No.13 of 1993 as an autonomous department under the Ministry of Health,
Social Development, Gender, Elderly and Children responsible for develop, maintain and manage an efficient and cost effective system of procurement,
storage and distribution of approved medicines and medical supplies required for use by the public health services as the Ministry of Health, Social Development,
Gender, Elderly and Children may from time to time approve.
